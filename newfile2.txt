Ethereum Advantages :

Smart & Transparent : Automated contracts, publicly viewable

Decentralized & Flexible : No central control, supports tokens and apps

Community & Standards : Strong developer base, popular token standards like ERC-20

Ethereum 2.0 Upgrade : Lower energy use, better scalability


Scalability & Fees: Slow during congestion, high transaction costs

Complexity & Security: Difficult development, risk of smart contract exploits

Competition & Legal Issues: Rival platforms and uncertain regulations
